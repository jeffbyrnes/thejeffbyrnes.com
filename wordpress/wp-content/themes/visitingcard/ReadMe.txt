How to install:
=======================================
1. Unpack the zip archive on your local hard drive
2. Upload the theme to the themes folder on your server ( wp-content/themes)
3. Go to Wp-Admin > Appearance > Themes and activate LiveTwit theme
5. Go to Wp-Admin > Appearance > Current Theme Options where you can do all the settings of this theme.
7. You're done, have fun.
